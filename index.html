The Program is best suited for

Aspiring Product Managers

Those who are looking to break into the field of product management will find Product Lantern's coursesinvaluable. The comprehensive curriculum covers essential topics and provides practical insights to helpbeginners build a strong foundation in product management principles and practices.

Current Product Managers

Professionals already working in product management roles can benefit from Product Lantern's courses to enhance their skills, stay updated on industry trends, and refine their strategies. Whether they are seeking to advance in their careers or improve their effectiveness in their current roles.

Tech Enthusiasts

The "Tech For Product" program is ideal for individuals who have a passion for technology and want totransition into product management roles within tech-driven industries. This program equips learnerswith the technical knowledge and understanding necessary to excel in product management roles in therapidly evolving tech landscape.

Individuals Seeking Mentorship

The "Product Mentorship" program is perfect for those who desire personalised guidance and support from seasoned professionals in the product management field. Mentees can benefit from the insights, advice, and career guidance provided by experienced mentors to accelerate their growth and development as product managers.

Career Changers

Individuals looking to transition into product management from other roles or industries can leverage
Product Lantern's resources to acquire the necessary skills and knowledge. The courses and mentorship
opportunities offered by Product Lantern can help career changers make a successful transition into the
field of product management.
